About Us

AMD is a pioneering semiconductor company with over five decades of innovation in computing technology. From multi-core processors to cutting-edge Radeon™ GPUs and Ryzen™ processors, we're committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in computing. Driven by our core values of integrity, excellence, and teamwork, AMD fosters a culture of collaboration and inclusivity. Trusted by millions of customers worldwide, we provide unparalleled performance for gamers, creativity for content creators, and scalable solutions for enterprises. Join us as we shape the future of technology together.

Our journey began in 1969 when Jerry Sanders, along with a group of engineers, founded AMD with a vision to innovate and disrupt the semiconductor industry. Since then, AMD has continuously pushed the boundaries of computing technology, pioneering advancements such as the first 64-bit x86 processor, the introduction of multi-core processors, and the development of high-performance GPUs for gaming and professional workloads.

Today, AMD is at the forefront of innovation, driving advancements in gaming, artificial intelligence, data centers, and beyond. Our dedication to delivering high-performance, energy-efficient solutions has earned us the trust of customers across various industries, making AMD a global leader in computing technology.
